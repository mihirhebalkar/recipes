Pasta

Description

Pasta is an italian dish maybe, can be cooked in many sauces like white,red,alfredo,pesto, also pasta itself has various shapes like macaroni, penne,etc. It tastes good if cooked well and is usually accompanied with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Money
  • Access to functioning kitchen
  • Pasta 0.5 kg
  • Readymade Pasta Sauce
  • Common sense
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Take a saucepan, add water and boil
  2. Add pasta and salt, until it is soft enough
  3. Take pasta out, remove water
  4. Add sauce and ingredients given in sauce's label
  5. Follow the steps in sauce label
  6. Eat and enjoy
